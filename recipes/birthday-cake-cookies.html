Birthday Cake Sugar Cookies

Photo by: Gio

A birthday cake sugar cookie is a delightful treat that captures the essence of a birthday cake in a compact, handheld form. This soft and chewy sugar cookie is infused with the sweet and buttery flavors reminiscent of a classic birthday cake. It is often adorned with colorful sprinkles or confetti, adding a festive touch to its appearance.
The cookie dough is carefully crafted to achieve the perfect balance of sweetness and texture. Baked to perfection, the cookie maintains a tender interior while developing a slightly crisp outer edge. With each bite, you'll experience the nostalgic flavors of vanilla and butter, reminiscent of a freshly baked birthday cake.
The birthday cake sugar cookie is a popular choice for celebrations, offering all the joy and nostalgia of a traditional birthday cake in a conveniently handheld form. Whether enjoyed as a standalone treat or as part of a dessert spread, this delightful cookie is sure to bring smiles and add a touch of sweetness to any birthday celebration.

Ingredients:

  • 8 tbsp Butter(soft)
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 2 Egg Yolks
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 tsp Almond Extract
  • 1 1/2 cups Cake Flour
  • 1/2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 cup Sprinkles

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Cream butter and sugar than add yolks, vanilla and almond extract
  3. In bowl mix flour, baking powder and salt
  4. Scoop and coat in sprinkles
  5. Bake for 9-11 mins
