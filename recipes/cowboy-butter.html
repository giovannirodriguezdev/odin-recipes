Cowboy Butter

Photo by: Gio

Cowboy butter is a flavorful and indulgent compound butter that pays homage to the bold and robust flavors of the Wild West. Combining rich ingredients like butter, garlic, herbs, and spices, cowboy butter packs a punch of savory goodness.
This velvety-smooth butter is infused with a harmonious blend of ingredients that add depth and complexity to any dish. The combination of melted butter, minced garlic, fresh herbs like parsley and thyme, along with a hint of zesty citrus, creates a tantalizing flavor profile that perfectly complements grilled meats, roasted vegetables, or even a simple slice of bread.
The name "cowboy butter" evokes images of open campfires, rustic landscapes, and hearty cowboy feasts. Its robust and savory nature adds a touch of Western charm to your culinary creations. Whether used as a marinade, finishing sauce, or melted atop a sizzling steak, cowboy butter adds a burst of flavor that will transport your taste buds to the untamed frontier.
Indulge in the essence of the Wild West with cowboy butter, a delectable condiment that infuses your dishes with a smoky, buttery richness, reminiscent of a cowboy's hearty meal under the stars.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup butter(soft)
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1 tbsp Garlic(minced)
  • 1 Shallot(minced)
  • 2 tbsp Dijon Mustard
  • 1 tbsp Horseradish
  • 1/4 tsp Paprika
  • Pinch of Cayenne Pepper
  • 2 tbsp Parsley(chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Green Onion(chopped)
  • Salt and Pepper to Taste

Direction:

  1. By hand or in a mixer combine all ingredients together and mix well.
